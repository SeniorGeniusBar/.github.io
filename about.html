About Senior Genius Bar

Why I started this project and how I hope it helps.

Meet Aiden

Hi, I'm Aiden O'Neill, a local high school student. I started Senior Genius Bar to help older adults feel more comfortable using technology.

I enjoy breaking confusing problems into simple steps and helping people become more independent.

Why I Started It

I noticed that simple tasks—like starting a FaceTime call, sending a photo, changing text size, or connecting to Wi‑Fi—can become frustrating when instructions move too quickly.

Senior Genius Bar is meant to provide calm, patient support and easy resources seniors can use between in-person visits.
