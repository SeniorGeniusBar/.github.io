About Senior Genius Bar

Why I started this project and how I hope it helps.

Meet Aiden

Hi, I'm Aiden O'Neill, a local high school student. Senior Genius Bar is a genuine passion of mine — I've seen firsthand the difference a little patience and clear instruction can make, with my older relatives, my neighbors, my boss, and the seniors I volunteer with.

I enjoy breaking confusing problems into simple steps and helping people become more independent with the technology they already own.

Why I Started It

Over and over, I noticed the same thing: it's rarely that the technology itself is too confusing, or that someone "can't" learn it. Anything unfamiliar just feels a little stressful at first — what actually helps is having someone willing to meet you where you are and explain it clearly, patiently, one step at a time.

Senior Genius Bar is meant to provide calm, patient support and easy resources seniors can use between in-person visits.

How I Try to Teach

I've always paid close attention to small details that often go unnoticed — and I think that's what makes step-by-step teaching work. I try never to skip a step, even one that seems obvious, since it's usually the skipped step that causes the most confusion.
