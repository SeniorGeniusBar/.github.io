Request Help Between Visits

This support is currently for Atria residents and their families only.

If you need help before the next in-person session, you can send a request. Aiden will reply when he is available.

  1. Tell Aiden your name and whether you are a resident or family member.
  2. Tell him what device you need help with.
  3. Briefly describe the problem or question.
  4. Do not include passwords, banking information, medical information, or private account details.

Important Safety Notes

  • Aiden will never ask for your passwords.
  • Please do not share banking, medical, Social Security, or private account information.
  • This is basic technology help, not emergency, medical, financial, or legal advice.
  • If something seems urgent or unsafe, please ask Atria staff or a trusted family member for help.

About Aiden

Hi, I'm Aiden O'Neill. I'm a local high school student who started Senior Genius Bar to help seniors feel more confident using technology.

I like helping people break confusing tech problems into simple steps. My goal is to help residents stay connected to family, friends, and the moments that matter most.
