Have a question between visits? Send it my way and I'll write back as soon as I can. This is just for Atria residents and their families.

  1. Tell me your name.
  2. Let me know what device you need help with.
  3. Briefly describe the problem or question — as much or as little detail as you like.

Important Safety Notes

  • Aiden will never ask for your passwords.
  • Please do not share banking, medical, Social Security, or private account information — with Aiden or anyone who contacts you unexpectedly.
  • This is basic technology help, not emergency, medical, financial, or legal advice.

Ask anything — no question is too small

Common Questions

Do I need to know anything about computers first?

Not at all. Most people who reach out have never used a smartphone or tablet before. We start wherever you are.

What if I forget my password?

Bring the device anyway. We can often help you reset it together, or talk through safe ways to keep track of passwords going forward.

Can a family member come with me?

Of course. Family members are always welcome to reach out on your behalf or join in on a call.
