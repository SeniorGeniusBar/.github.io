Free Tech Guides

Simple instructions written for clarity and confidence.

Tip: Can't find an app on your device? Swipe down or tap the search icon and type the app's name — that trick works for finding almost anything. And if any step below doesn't quite match what you see, that's okay, devices vary a little. Ask Aiden if you get stuck, any time.

How to Make Text Bigger on an iPhone
  1. Open Settings.
  2. Tap Display & Brightness.
  3. Tap Text Size.
  4. Move the slider to the right.
  5. For even larger text, go to Settings → Accessibility → Display & Text Size.
How to Start a FaceTime Call
  1. Open FaceTime.
  2. Tap New FaceTime.
  3. Enter the person's name, number, or email.
  4. Tap the person, then tap the green FaceTime button.
How to Send a Photo by Text
  1. Open Messages.
  2. Open the conversation.
  3. Tap the plus sign or photo icon.
  4. Select a photo.
  5. Tap the blue send arrow.
How to Connect to Wi‑Fi
  1. Open Settings.
  2. Tap Wi‑Fi.
  3. Turn Wi‑Fi on.
  4. Tap the network name.
  5. Enter the password if asked.
Scam Protection Basics
  • Do not click links from unknown senders.
  • Never share passwords or verification codes.
  • Pause when a message creates urgency or fear.
  • Ask a trusted person before sending money or information.
Password Tips
  • Use a different password for important accounts.
  • Use long phrases that are easier to remember.
  • Do not send passwords by email or text.
  • Turn on two-step verification when possible.
