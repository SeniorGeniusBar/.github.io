Helpful Apps

  • FaceTime — my favorite for seeing family's faces, not just hearing their voices. Already on your iPhone, free, no download needed.
  • Google Meet — the Android equivalent of FaceTime, works great for video calls too.
    Get it: App Store · Google Play
  • Medisafe — never wonder again if you took your medicine today. Free reminders, right on your phone.
    Get it: App Store · Google Play
  • Find My (iPhone) or Find My Device (Android) — lost your phone in the couch cushions? This finds it in seconds. Already built in, no download needed.
  • Your phone's built-in Magnifier — turns your camera into a magnifying glass, perfect for menus and mail. Already built in, no download needed.

More suggestions will be added thoughtfully over time.
